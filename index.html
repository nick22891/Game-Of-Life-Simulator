N.B. This wikipedia page explains the original game concept better than I can.

1) Click anywhere on the map to start adding boxes to the grid.

2) Each click toggles the box between life and death (i.e. makes a box appear or disappear)

3) When you're ready, hit "Start Game!".

4) It's a good idea to stop the game before making any changes to the map.
